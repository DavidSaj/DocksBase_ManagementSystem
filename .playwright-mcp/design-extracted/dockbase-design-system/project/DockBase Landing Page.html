What DocksBase does

Everything a harbor needs, in one place.

Built for the pace of working harbors — fast, reliable, and calm under pressure.

Berth Management

Real-time occupancy grid across all piers and slips. Assign, transfer, and release berths with one click. Know what's available the moment it is.

Bookings & Arrivals

Take advance bookings, manage walk-in arrivals, and track departures. Full booking history per vessel, with payment status and outstanding invoices.

Service Scheduling

Coordinate fueling, pump-out, maintenance, and provisioning requests. Assign dock crew, track completion, and log service history per vessel.

Interactive Port Map

Visual map of your harbor with live berth status overlaid. See occupied, available, and maintenance berths at a glance — no spreadsheet required.

Vessel Registry

Full vessel database with owner contacts, specifications, insurance status, and arrival/departure logs. Quick lookup by name, IMO number, or owner.

Weather & Tides

Live conditions dashboard with wind, swell, visibility, and pressure. Tide forecasts integrated into your operations view so you're never caught out.
